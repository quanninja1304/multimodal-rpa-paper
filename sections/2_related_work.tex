\section{Related Work}

\subsection{Document Understanding}

LayoutLM~\cite{xu2020layoutlm} encodes text with bounding-box positions for form understanding, but needs fine-tuning per layout. Donut~\cite{kim2022ocr} skips OCR and decodes from images directly, but is expensive and needs training data. Our system uses Gemini 2.5 Flash to process raw image bytes natively — no OCR, no fine-tuning.

\subsection{Vietnamese ASR}

Vietnamese is tonal with six tone classes and has limited training data. PhoWhisper~\cite{le2024phowhisper} fine-tunes Whisper~\cite{radford2023whisper} on 800+ hours of Vietnamese speech. VietASR~\cite{zhuo2025vietasr} combines HuBERT~\cite{hsu2021hubert} with Zipformer~\cite{yao2024zipformer}. Both struggle with mixed Vietnamese-English product names. Instead of a dedicated ASR module, we send raw audio bytes to Gemini for joint transcription and extraction, with phonetic correction rules in the prompt.

\subsection{LLM-Based Extraction}

Traditional NER (e.g., BiLSTM-CRF~\cite{lample2016neural}) needs labeled data per schema. LLMs like Gemini~\cite{reid2024gemini} can extract structured data zero-shot from a prompt. The downside is cost and latency per document. We use the LLM for two tasks: multimodal extraction and product query normalization, both with Pydantic schema validation and batching (up to 10 queries per call).

\subsection{Hybrid Retrieval}

BM25~\cite{robertson2009probabilistic} does exact matching but misses synonyms. Sentence-BERT~\cite{reimers2019sentence} captures semantics but confuses similar specs (e.g., 8W vs 9W). Hybrid fusion~\cite{cormack2009reciprocal} combines both. We use Weighted Score Fusion in Qdrant~\cite{qdrant}: Vietnamese Sentence-BERT (dense) + learned BM25 via fastembed (sparse), with $\alpha = 0.7$ for dense weight. An LLM-predicted category filter narrows the search space; if it returns nothing, we fall back to unfiltered search.
